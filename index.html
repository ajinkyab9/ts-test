Services

Injection Moulding

At TS we can produce any plastic injection moulded part from miniature mouldings to large scale mouldings and have the flexibility to allow for short run production to high volume production requirements.

Services

Blow Moulding

Our facility is equipped with a wide range of modern micro-processor controlled blow moulding machines, which are capable of delivering precise and exceptional quality blow moulded products.

Services

3D Printing

We pride ourselves in the 3D printing department. Our facility harbours modern and accurate 3D printing machines which can turn any prototype to a 3D printed miracle. We also have the capacity of mass producing 3D printed products.
